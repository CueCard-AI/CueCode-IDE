# Business Support

For billing and business support (account setup, invoices, organization questions), email [support@cuecode.dev](mailto:support@cuecode.dev). Business support is prioritized relative to other support channels.

For general questions, email [support@cuecode.dev](mailto:support@cuecode.dev).

## Open-source issues

Questions and bugs about CueCode itself (features, extensions, language support, crashes) go through the main CueCode project on [GitHub](https://github.com/zed-industries/zed/issues).
